Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how White Stag Digital LLC ("White Stag Digital," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the internal software applications we operate, including the "Trademark Monitor" and related Meta Marketing API integrations (collectively, the "Service"). The Service is operated solely by White Stag Digital and is not made available to external end users.

1. Scope

The Service consists of internal tools used by White Stag Digital and its authorized personnel to manage and monitor advertising activity on advertising platforms (including Meta Platforms, Inc.) for advertising accounts owned by or directly authorized to White Stag Digital. The Service does not host consumer-facing accounts and does not solicit personal information from the public.

2. Information We Access

Using credentials issued to us by advertising platforms, the Service may access the following categories of information:

  • Advertising-account metadata (account IDs, campaign and ad set names, budgets, status)
  • Ad creative metadata (image hashes, video IDs, ad copy, links)
  • Aggregated advertising performance metrics (spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, ROAS)
  • Publicly available advertising data from the Meta Ad Library
  • Operational logs generated by the Service itself (request timestamps, error messages, job status)

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or other personally identifiable information from members of the public through the Service.

3. How We Use Information

Information accessed through the Service is used exclusively for the internal business purposes of White Stag Digital, including:

  • Creating, managing, and reporting on advertising campaigns we operate
  • Monitoring third-party advertising activity for potential trademark or brand-protection issues
  • Generating internal performance reports for White Stag Digital and its directly authorized clients
  • Maintaining the security and reliability of the Service

4. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade information accessed through the Service. Information may be shared only:

  • With the advertising-account owner to whom the information already belongs
  • With White Stag Digital's internal personnel under standard confidentiality obligations
  • With infrastructure providers strictly to the extent required to operate the Service (e.g., hosting, error logging)
  • When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process

5. Data Retention

Operational logs and cached advertising data are retained only as long as needed to operate the Service and produce internal reports, after which they are deleted or aggregated. Source data on advertising platforms (such as Meta) remains governed by those platforms' own retention policies.

6. Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the credentials and data the Service handles, including environment- scoped secrets, encrypted transport (HTTPS) for all API traffic, and access restricted to authorized White Stag Digital personnel.

7. Meta Platforms

The Service interacts with Meta Platforms, Inc. via the Meta Marketing API and the Meta Ad Library. Our use of information obtained from Meta is governed by the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We do not use Meta data for purposes outside of those Terms.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly access information about children through the Service.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be reflected in that date.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to chris@whitestagdigital.com.
